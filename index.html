About Me

Be who you are and say what you feel, because those who mind don't matter, and those who matter don't mind.

Perhaps more than the business itself, what excites me most of all is working with a committed, creative, and talented team. I am driven and inspired by the momentum of those I work with, and seek to drive and inspire them in return.

I am passionate about user experience and product development. These areas allow me to both find fulfillment in connecting with users, and challenge me to tackle new issues every day. I thrive in a fast-paced environment, working with all divisions within a company to help drive a unified vision and extraordinary success. I have built a career guiding talented developers and designers, helping them build innovative, first-class products. To round out my qualifications as product director, I recently embarked on a three-month programming bootcamp to add the full MEAN stack to my toolset.
